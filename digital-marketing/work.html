FOOD WALK

The 5th of February 2016 saw the organisation of a food walk at Amar Colony by Markitiers in collaboration with Trending Foodies and Same Swag. We had a completely unexpected and warm reception from the nearby restaurants as well as the street sellers. At "Sharma Chat Bhandar," we held a "gol gappa eating competition," in which everyone from children to aunts and members to non-members took part. The next stop on our tour was "Hunger Strike," which is the most well-known location in Amar Colony and offers a wide selection of experimental foods. The tandoori momos at this location are the best; you will undoubtedly become addicted to them.
