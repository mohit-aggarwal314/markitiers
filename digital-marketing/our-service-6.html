Events And Outdoor Advertisement

We are the creative event management business you have been looking for if you need complete meetings, corporate events, or event management services. We can assist you in achieving your objectives if you have a project that needs event planning that is coming up.

The outdoor advertisement is one of the most well-known forms of advertising in Delhi. There are numerous ways to reach the target audience while using outdoor media. Given that it is one of the first and most widely used advertising channels, it has a history of getting brands the desired response. It is the most adaptable medium because countless possibilities are available to run the campaign well within the budget depending on their position, illumination, display material, etc.

B). Delhi’s Biggest Blogger Meet:- The second iteration of Delhi's largest Bloggers carnival was successfully held on February 4 at Founders café thanks to Markitiers' collaboration with Delhi Pedia and Events Mosaic. Blossom Kochhar and Global Gadgets jointly powered the event.
